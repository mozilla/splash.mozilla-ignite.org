Ignite Apps Challenge

Imagine and build smarter, screaming fast apps— apps that can change the lives of your sister, your grandmother, and millions of people. We're offering support and funding to help you solve real world problems.

  1. the GENI Sandboxa 1 Gbps, highly programmable network where you can build, test, and play.
  2. The Mozilla communitythe Mozilla Developer Network, WebFWD, and all our experience on how to develop for the open web.
  3. $500k in fundingto help the best ideas get built, quickly.
