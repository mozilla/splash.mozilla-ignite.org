The Mozilla Ignite Apps Challenge

1 Gigabit per second. Up and down. 250x faster than what we have now. What could you do with that speed? Mozilla, the National Science Foundation, and the White House are launching an open innovation challenge to find out. And we're giving you access to 3 things to help:

  1. The GENI SandboxAn existing 1 Gbps network where you can build, test, and play.
  2. The Mozilla communitythe Mozilla Developer Network, WebFWD, and all our experience on how to develop for the open web.
  3. FundingAnd $500,000 in funding to help the best ideas get built.
